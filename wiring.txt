Wiring of the TFT1.8-Display and the Raspberry Pi


Display                Raspberry Pi

VCC ------------------ 5V Power      (Pin 2)
GND ------------------ Ground        (Pin 6)
SCL ------------------ GPIO11 (SCLK) (Pin 23)
SDA ------------------ GPIO10 (MOSI) (Pin 19)
RS/DC ---------------- GPIO24        (Pin 18)
RES ------------------ GPIO25        (Pin 22)
CS ------------------- GPIO8 (CE0)   (Pin 24)
MISO  not connected (for SD-Card)
SCLK  not connected (for SD-Card)
MOSI  not connected (for SD-Card)
CS    not connected (for SD-Card)
